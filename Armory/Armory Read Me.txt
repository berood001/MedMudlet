This package will read items you are wearing, in your inventory, and in your locker. It saves to a file 
which can be read by the CharacterSheet.html file that gets loaded when this package is installed. No
need to search for it, a link is provided after running one of these commands:
apparmory - reads your equipment and saves
applocker - reads your locker and saves *you have to be next to your locker of course or its just blank*
appinv - reads your inventory and saves

* You will need to identify items first, else you will just get the name and unidentified
* You should always have detect invisible on before running, else you will have missing items OR it may just say "something"

Why use this? If you are running around with 9 characters and 3-4 alts holding gear the reason is pretty clear. It's difficult to quickly
search through all your gear.
